PolyPlanner is the software used by all CalPoly Students to plan their 4 year course plan. 2 years ago I started trying to redesign it.

Step 1: Analysis
My Sophomore year I wrote a 15 page design analysis of PolyPlanner.

TLDR: IT SUCKED. Too many useless & distracting elements and too many hidden items

Check out my Design Analysis

Step 2: Join FlowChamp team
Another group of students were building there own version of PolyPlanner called FlowChamp. I joined their team as the lead designer. They had a great product but lacked an understanding of usability.

Step 3: Redesign
Me and the lead developer on FlowChamp both took a UI design class together. In class we worked on improving FlowChamp’s UI. We ran over 30 usertests, where we found usability issues and fixed them.
